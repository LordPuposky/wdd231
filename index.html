My technical stack includes HTML5, CSS3 (including Tailwind CSS and Bootstrap), JavaScript (ES6+), React.js, Node.js, Express.js, and both NoSQL (MongoDB) and SQL (PostgreSQL) databases. I seek remote opportunities as a Frontend or Full Stack Developer, with teams that value innovation, learning, and collaboration. With bilingual English–Spanish communication skills and a growth mindset, I stand out for my proactive, accountable work style and positive leadership.
