Discover Bogotá

Bogotá is Colombia’s lively capital, full of culture, history, and diversity. Here you can discover some fun facts, impressive numbers, and must-see places!

Interesting Facts

  • Bogotá is located at 2,640 meters (8,660 feet) above sea level, making it one of the highest capitals in the world.
  • The city was founded on August 6, 1538, by Gonzalo Jiménez de Quesada.
  • Bogotá has over 7.5 million inhabitants, making it the largest city in Colombia.
  • The city boasts more than 400 km of bike paths, the largest cycle route network in Latin America.
  • La Candelaria historic district is famous for its cobblestone streets, colorful buildings, and lively street art.
  • Bogotá’s Gold Museum has more than 55,000 pieces of gold from the pre-Columbian era.
  • Each Sunday and public holiday, Bogotá's “Ciclovía” closes main roads to cars and opens them to bikes and pedestrians.
