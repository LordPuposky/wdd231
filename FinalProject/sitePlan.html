Site Plan

FutureBank Insights - Digital Banking Trends

Site Name

FutureBank Insights

The name “FutureBank Insights” was chosen to reflect a forward-looking platform focused on analyzing and explaining the most important digital banking trends for the next five years. It communicates a combination of financial innovation (“FutureBank”) and curated analysis (“Insights”) aimed at users, companies, and developers.

Example domain (optional): futurebankinsights.com

Site Purpose Statement

The purpose of the site is to provide a compact and informative platform where users can explore the key innovations transforming digital banking. The website will highlight emerging financial technologies such as AI-driven finance, biometric authentication, real-time payments, decentralized identity, hyper-personalization, embedded banking, and banking-as-a-service.

The project will consist of three main pages: a Home page that introduces the project and summarizes the main technological forces; a Trends Dashboard that displays dynamic data loaded from JSON about major industry trends; and a User Preferences & Resources page where visitors can save their favorite trends using localStorage, submit feedback or newsletter signup through a form, and access additional fintech resources. This site demonstrates modern web development skills while presenting meaningful insights into the future of financial services.

Scenarios

  • What technologies are shaping the future of digital banking?
  • How are financial institutions using AI and biometrics to improve user experience and security?
  • Which digital banking trends have the highest growth and adoption potential over the next five years?
  • How can I identify which digital banking trends are most relevant for my company or professional profile?

Typography

  • Headings: Montserrat (h1, h2, h3, navigation titles).
  • Body Text: Inter (paragraph text, lists, descriptions, form text).

Montserrat will be used to give a modern, bold and fintech-oriented look to the main headings, while Inter will provide clean, highly readable body text for longer explanations and data descriptions across all pages.

Testing

  • HTML Validation: W3C Markup Validator to check for valid and well-structured HTML.
  • Accessibility: WAVE Web Accessibility Evaluation Tool to detect accessibility issues.
  • Best Practices, SEO & Performance: Google PageSpeed Insights or Chrome DevTools Lighthouse.
  • Color Contrast: WebAIM Contrast Checker to ensure text and UI elements meet contrast guidelines.
