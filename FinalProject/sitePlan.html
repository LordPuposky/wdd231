FutureBank Insights - Site Plan

Site Name

FutureBank Insights - The name represents a forward-looking financial technology platform focused on helping users understand the most important digital banking trends shaping the next five years.

Site Purpose

The purpose of the site is to provide a compact and informative platform where users can explore the key innovations transforming digital banking. The website will highlight emerging financial technologies such as AI-driven finance, biometric authentication, real-time payments, decentralized identity, hyper-personalization, and banking-as-a-service. The project demonstrates modern web development skills while presenting meaningful insights into the future of financial services.

Scenarios

  • What are the most influential digital banking trends projected for the next five years?
  • How can companies prepare for technologies like AI automation or biometric authentication?

Color Scheme

#0043A4 - Primary
#0A0F1F - Header/Footer
#FFFFFF - Background
#00C6A7 - Highlights/Icons

  • #0A0F1F - Header, footer, and navigation background.
  • #FFFFFF - Main body background.
  • #00C6A7 - Accent color for interactive UI elements.

Typography

  • Poppins - Used for headings and titles.
  • Inter - Used for body text and paragraphs.
